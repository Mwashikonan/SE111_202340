ABOUT I N C E P T I O N

Inception is a 2010 sci-fi adventure, where dream-sharing technology exists in the world. Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) learned to take advantage of this by stealing secrets from powerful corporations. After many misfortunes, he puts together a team to do one last job to get back home. But this last dream was nothing they were expecting. Will Cobb and his team finish this job and get to go home?
